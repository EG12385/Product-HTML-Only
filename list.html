Creating a contact list

This is one of the first steps to get you sending your email campaigns.

To create a list, you need to have new or existing contacts, leads or referals to include in a group, or in more than one group, or rather lists.
The start to your email communications, will be the list of persons you created that will receive your notifications, this will serve as your audience.

To create your list, the following steps should be taken:

  1. On the menu panel, from the 'Audience' dro-down list, select 'Lists https://mailer.ictglobe.com/lists'
  2. Your lists will appear under 'My lists'. You can edit an existing list, or create a new list.
  3. To create a new list select 'Create list ' https://mailer.ictglobe.com/lists/create
  4. Edit the the following list specifications: Identity, Contact Information and Settings, then save.
  5. Once saved, the list will be accessible from the lists, from where additional settings and functions can be added.
Once saved, the list will be available from you lists created.
