A Brief Overview of the Menu

The following is available from Dashboard: https://mailer.ictglobe.com/
  1. Credits Uded:

    The view provides you with a summary of the credits used, and available. Each mail, once send equals 1 credit, regardless of the activity log related to the send mail. If, for example, you have 1000 email subscribers, you might need at least 3000 to 4000 credits per month, dependant on the amount of mails, and campaigns processed.

  2. Recently sent Campaigns:

    Statistics provides harts and tables, a visual overview of you marketing metrics. Success rates can be viewed, from opened to reported actions per sent campaigns.

    Open, and click rate, measures how many delivered emails were opened by your subscribers, and how many recipients clicked on one of the links in your email campaign.

    The ‘Open log’, provides additional information such as: Recipient, IP Address, Campaign, Sending Server, Area and Created date. Under ‘Sending logs’ you will find a list of logs for different criteria.

  3. 24-Hour Performance:

    This report measures the specific campaigns subscriber activity for the last 24 hours.

  4. Other Logs on the Dashboard:

    Top countries by opens

    List Growth

    Activity Log

    Personalization of your user profile and preferances.

The following is available from Campaigns: https://mailer.ictglobe.com/campaigns

Campaigns, provides a list of your campaigns created, processed, and a list of additional actions to choose from.

The following is available from Automations: https://mailer.ictglobe.com/automation

Using the build-in automation, you can create new, or edit existing automation triggers, to perform task in an automatic sequence.

Each trigger can be reviewed, changed or removed by selecting it from the list.
