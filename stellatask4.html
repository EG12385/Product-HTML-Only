Table 1.
Feature Description
Spam Testing One of the major obstacles to success with email marketing is spam filters. We test your deliverables against spam filters, ensuring that you do not have unavoidable issues, improving your deliverability on: open rate, click actions, and purchases.
Mobile Optimization With the new generation and social changes, more users opens emails and messages from mobile or other devices. The Email Marketing Application comes with build-in design features, for email, templates and forms, which allows optimization and design for a variety of screen sizes.
Robust Analytics Digital marketing is all about data, including email-marketing metrics such as open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate, and bounce rate. The software comes with build-in testing tools improving your marketing. For each list created, you can add segments of data that you would like to monitor with specific conditions.
Automation Tools Automation triggers helps you to set up automatic sequences, from abandoned carts to a simple welcome message, freeing your time to focus on other areas of your business. Build- in automations comes with a list of actions that starts the automation process for a given campaign.
Scalable Pricing Why invest in an expensive enterprise-level plan from the start? We offer multiple subscription tiers at affordable prices, allowing you to upgrade as your business grows. You have multiple suitable message limits to choose from in case you need to scale.
Third-party Integration You might already have applications and subscription services, with the need for an email-marketing platform that can fit into your existing organizations technical stack. Integration is one of the optional functions, allowing you to connect you home page, enabling subscription popup forms from your business website.
