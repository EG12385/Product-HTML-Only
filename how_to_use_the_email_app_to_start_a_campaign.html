How to use the email app to start a campaign

You will learn how to create an E-mail campaign in just a view minutes!

In order to create a new E-mail campaign you need to have an 'audience' list that will be included in your campaign

Once you add your contact list. You will be able to create your first campaign. We'll work you through the steps.

  1. From the menu, select
  2. You can view existing, or create new email campaigns by selecting, '+ Create campaign'
  3. When creating a campaign you have two options: Regular or Plain TextSelect one of the options provided
  4. Choose one or more lists/segments for sending email
  5. Create your email from scratch, or use the simple to use pre-build templates
  6. After creating the template, review, edit or add additional attachments to your mail
  7. Happy with your mail creation? You can now schedule the campaign by confirming the delivery date and time to launch your new campaign
  8. The final step is to confirm your campaign, is to preview or send a 'test' email
  9. You can now measure your campaign metrics with the build-in statistic reports
