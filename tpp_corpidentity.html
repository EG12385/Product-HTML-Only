The Presence Platform_Corporate Identity

Once you have a logo, your company's Corporate Identity is essential.

Your corporate identity helps everyone in your company know how to use the company artwork and keep it all in line with the company image.

The following four elements, forms part of your corporate identity:

  1. Logo Design

    As a standout visual signifier, the company logo is the most obvious and should act to identify your organization instantly.

  2. Fonts & Typeface

    The style, shape, weight and spacing of a written word can mean a lot in the case of web content, business cards and email templates. Typographic style may be one of the most important elements appearing on emails, presentations, contracts, email signitures, brochures, stationaly and more.

  3. Imagery

    Images are powerful elements of your corporate identity, representing the company with the consistant feel that the photography and ilustrations have.

  4. Style Guidelines

    Include 'Dos and Dont's' in your brand style guides, to avoid any misinterpretation of the brand in the case were different visual assets are used.
